About Soda Springs

By Diego Gomez

The name of the city is for the hundreds of natural springs of carbonated water. It is known as the city of the Soda Springs Geyser. According to the 2010 census there are about 3,058 inhabitants and 830 families living in the city. It has several parks like Hooper Springs Park that has clear sparkling soda water. It is a prime attraction for more than 160 years.
