Example of Worker

Without the worker if we enter a sizable number, it going to take longer to process the answer and the browser will display a warning and you can't change the colors. The whole program greezes until the operation is complete.

Using workers the factors will be calculated even if its a big number, it will be done in the background and send a message that he is working on it while the colors of the background can be change.
